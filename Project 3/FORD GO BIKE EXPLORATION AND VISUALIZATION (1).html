Part I - San Francisco Bay Area Bike System Exploration¶

by Ukachi Agnes, Eze-Mbey¶

Introduction¶

The dataset to be analyzed includes information about individual rides made in a bike-sharing system covering the greater San Francisco Bay area. It contains start and end station of the bikes, start and end time of sharing, information on whether the user is a customer or susbcriber to the system, member gender and year of birth.
